About

Genesis Temtainment, founded in 2024, is a charity organisation
that specializes on stage arts. Creating a large experience a large
experience of art for the people of Aldershot and the United
Kingdom at large. We create a variety of performing arts for young people at age 8-18.

Mission

  • To create dramatic performances for the people of England
  • Briging people together to celebrate cultural forms
  • Creating great experiences with musical theatre
  • Create music/dance/drama for young people.
  • Promote diversity equality and lots more.

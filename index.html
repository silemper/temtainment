About

Genesis Temtainment, founded in 2024, is a charity organisation that specializes on stage arts. Creating a large experience a large experience of art for the people of Aldershot and the United Kingdom at large. We create a variety of performing arts for young people at age 8-18.

Mission

  • Creating great experiences with musical theatre.
